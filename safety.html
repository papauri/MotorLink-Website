Marketplace Safety

Protect yourself at every step. Use this checklist when buying, selling, test-driving, or making payments.

Buyer Checklist Seller Checklist Red Flags Emergency Help

Buyer Safety Checklist

  • Confirm registration details match the seller identity and vehicle papers.
  • Inspect in daylight and bring someone you trust for a second opinion.
  • Test all lights, brakes, electronics, and basic engine health before agreeing.
  • Do not send full payment before inspection and ownership document verification.
  • Use traceable payment channels and keep transfer receipts.
Tip: Meet in busy public locations and avoid isolated handover points.

Seller Safety Checklist

  • Never release the vehicle before payment is fully confirmed and cleared.
  • Remove sensitive documents and personal items before any test drive.
  • Verify buyer identity for high-value transactions.
  • Use documented communication channels and avoid cash-only pressure deals.
  • Immediately report abusive behavior, threats, or fake proof of payment.
Tip: Keep a written handover confirmation signed by both parties.

Red Flags to Watch For

Stop the deal immediately if you see these signs:

  • Seller refuses inspection or document checks.
  • Pressure to pay quickly with no verification window.
  • Price is far below market value without clear reason.
  • Conflicting ownership details across documents and messages.
  • Requests to move communication to unknown or suspicious channels.
