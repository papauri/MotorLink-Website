Listing Policy: Keep Ads Unique!

One Active Listing Per Car

To keep our marketplace clean and searches relevant, please maintain one active listing per vehicle. This helps buyers find what they're looking for quickly and prevents confusion. Duplicate listings will be automatically detected and removed.

Why Unique Listings Matter

  • Better Search Results: Buyers see more variety, not the same car multiple times
  • More Views: Unique listings rank better and get more attention
  • Professional Appearance: Shows you're a serious, trustworthy seller
  • Faster Sales: Buyers trust listings that aren't duplicated
  • Fair Marketplace: Prevents unfair advantage through spam listings

How to Manage Your Listings

  • Before posting: Check if you already have a listing for the same vehicle
  • Need to update? Edit your existing listing instead of creating a new one
  • Car sold or changed? Delete or deactivate your old listing first
  • Different cars? Feel free to list each one - just keep them unique!
  • Price changes: Update your existing listing - don't create a new one

What Counts as a Duplicate?

A duplicate listing is when you have multiple active ads for the same vehicle with:

  • Same make, model, and year
  • Same or very similar mileage (within 1,000 km)
  • Same or very similar description (more than 70% identical)
  • Same photos or slightly modified ones
  • Posted by the same seller (you!)
  • Similar specifications (color, transmission, fuel type)

Our automated system checks for these similarities. If a potential duplicate is detected, you'll be notified and asked to verify before posting.

Strict Verification Process

All listings go through our verification process to ensure quality and accuracy:

  • Automatic Duplicate Detection: Our system scans for similar listings before allowing submission
  • Photo Verification: We check that photos match the car description
  • Price Reasonableness: Prices are compared to market values to prevent scams
  • Contact Information: Seller contact details are verified for authenticity
  • Review Time: Most listings are reviewed within 2-4 hours during business hours

Guest Posting Limits

To maintain quality while welcoming new users:

  • Guest users can post one active car listing
  • Want to list more? Create a free account - it takes 2 minutes!
  • Account holders get unlimited listings, message tracking, and verified badges
  • Guest listings undergo more thorough review to ensure quality

Consequences of Violating Listing Policy

  • First offense: Duplicate listings will be removed, and you'll receive a warning
  • Second offense: All your listings may be suspended for review
  • Repeated violations: Your account may be permanently banned
  • Spam behavior: Immediate account termination
