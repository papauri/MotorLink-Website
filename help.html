MotorLink Help Center

Quick guidance for buyers, sellers, and business accounts. Use these sections to solve common issues quickly, then contact our support team if you need direct help.

Buyers Sellers Businesses FAQs Contact Support

Buyer Help

  • Use filters (price, location, make/model) to narrow your shortlist quickly.
  • Prefer verified sellers and listings with complete photos and service history details.
  • Always inspect and test drive before payment. Meet in a safe, public place.
  • Report suspicious listings from the listing page so our team can review them quickly.

Seller Help

  • Use clear, current photos and include exact year, mileage, and condition.
  • Set a realistic price range and keep your contact details updated.
  • Avoid duplicate listings. Update your existing listing if details change.
  • Mark sold listings promptly so buyers always see current inventory.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does listing approval take?

Most listings are reviewed within business hours, often on the same day.

Can I edit my listing after posting?

Yes. Open your dashboard and update price, description, photos, and availability at any time.

How do I report fraud or suspicious behavior?

Use the report action on the listing page and include as many details as possible.
